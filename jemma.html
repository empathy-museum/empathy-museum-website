Learning disabilities nurse

Jemma started volunteering at a playscheme when she was 16, partly because she had experienced a period of ill-health and missed a lot of school. She says being a learning disabilities nurse is quite hard to describe because it’s so multifaceted, working across physical and mental health, in the community and in children’s services. Currently Jemma works at policy level, changing these systems to be more inclusive of people with learning disabilities.
